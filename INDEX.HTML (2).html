       Dedicated to A Dear Friend and Artist, The One and Only,  AMSheep! 
Starting as a Small Humble Artist please take a Moment and Follow Their Social Media.




First of All, Who is AMSheep? AMSheep is my friend and fellow Artist of course! They are a small original content creator with a small following. Over The years they have created art for their own original characters and have inspired many in their community be artistic.

AMSheep is also a story-teller and has made countless characters to fill up their world of both fantasy and terror. Each OC has a unique flair to them that seperates them from another leaving each new flavor a new exciting spark just waiting to be disocvered. If you are a fan of character design than this is the artist for you.

Make sure to check their content out for who knows? Maybe they can scratch that one very specific itch in your brain with what they got in stock.



Their Pronouns are They/Them >_<



AMSheep is a Content Creator, So the next Obvious question is likely going to be be "What kind of Content do they create"

AMSheep creates stories both based on fiction but also their reality, They like us, is capable of making entire worlds in their heads if they put in the time and effort. And these world's have characters and aspects of their universe that makes them interesting. They put you into a colorful world and you get the chance to wander around and also you get a chance to contribute to this by interacting with AMSheep on their pots.

Get Ready for a Thrill as this content creator LOVES many types of Horror and also comedty, A character may be a walking piece of meat born to be eaten but it has such silly googly eyes that you cannot just help but laugh!

"I had a dream that I was making a character and I suddenly woke up because I had gotten myself hyped up because I came up with a good idea for the design and then I realised that the character didn't exist. So I made it again in real life" -AMSheep.



Why Are They Here? Why not a Larger more popular artist with movies and shows to their name?

AMSheep is my valued friend and artist because they are just as great as those artists of time like Lemon Demon or Crane Wives, And they are honestly amazing. They are a member of the community and as they are a talented individual born of skill and time. AMSheep is an artist just the same and one I cherish to this day.

To all our artists, dedicating this page to AMSheep has given me the motivation to say as they say:

"All I did was use this color with this one, But it's giving me an idea for even more colors. Characters are hard to design but when you do it for a friend you suddenly have the energy to get up and do it. Do it for them."

-AMSHEEP, 2024
